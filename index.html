Retreat

The Pause Retreat



Presents intercessors with an extraordinary opportunity to revitalize, heal, and reconnect with their true selves. By taking time to pause, you can cultivate a deeper understanding of self-love, as emphasized in Matthew 22:39: to genuinely love others, we first need to embrace love for ourselves. This retreat addresses the signs of emotional and spiritual burnout that many intercessors face, offering effective strategies to rekindle joy and passion in your life. Discover the transformative power of stillness as you learn to quiet the mind and heart, enabling you to hear God's voice more clearly. Indulge in vital self-care experiences, including a soothing foot massage and an invigorating upper body massage. Additionally, benefit from a personalized one-on-one counseling session designed to support you on your intercession journey. Embrace this chance to rejuvenate your spirit and reignite your passion for prayer and service.



Retreat starts Friday at 7pm



As you arrive at the pause retreat, approach the experience with an open heart and a sense of anticipation, fully prepared to embrace new beginnings and transformative moments. Take a deep breath and allow yourself to let go of past concerns, understanding that this is a unique opportunity for personal growth and reflection. This is truly your time to take a PAUSE, recharge your spirit, and rediscover your innermost desires. Embrace the journey ahead with optimism and a willingness to explore the possibilities that await you.

Truth Telling



Removing the mask in a session that reveals our authentic selves is essential for healing. To do so, we must cleanse ourselves, release old hurts, and free ourselves from past wounds. On the Saturday night presents a perfect opportunity for us to cry, embrace and support one another in our healing journey. LET THE HEALING BEGIN!



Spa Day



Treat yourself to a rejuvenating spa day this Saturday during the retreat, where you can unwind with soothing treatments and indulgent relaxation options. Embrace this opportunity to pamper yourself—there’s no need to feel guilty. Intercessors will help restore balance to your life. Prioritize self-care and take care of YOU!
